Privacy Policy

Oneiro Collection LLC — Operations Platform
Last updated: May 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Oneiro Collection LLC ("we", "us", "Oneiro") collects and uses information through its internal Operations Platform (the "Platform"). The Platform is a private, employee-facing application used to coordinate field operations, document management, payroll inputs, and invoicing for Oneiro's roadway-striping work. It is not a public-facing or consumer product.

1. Information we collect

  • Work Order data — job identifiers, locations, dates, quantities, and status information related to roadway-striping work orders.
  • Crew sign-in records — employee name, classification, time in/out, and hours worked, captured at job sites for payroll and reporting.
  • Photos and signatures — uploaded by crew at job sites to evidence completed work or sign-off; these stay in Oneiro's storage and are not shared publicly.
  • Contractor / customer records — names of prime contractors and related contract metadata, used to route invoices and reports.
  • Authentication tokens — short-lived API access tokens and rotating refresh tokens issued by integrated third-party services (e.g. QuickBooks Online), stored only as needed to perform automated tasks.

2. How we use this information

Information collected through the Platform is used solely to operate Oneiro's business: dispatching crews, tracking job progress, generating compliance documents, paying employees, and producing invoices to contractors. We do not sell, rent, or use this information for marketing.

3. Third-party services

To deliver these features, the Platform interfaces with a small number of vendors on Oneiro's behalf:

  • Google Workspace — Drive and Sheets used as the system of record for work order data and document storage.
  • Intuit QuickBooks Online — used to create and manage invoices owed to Oneiro by its prime contractors. The Platform reads customer and item metadata and writes invoice records on Oneiro's behalf.
  • Railway — application hosting provider.
  • Anthropic — used in limited contexts to assist with parsing of scanned work-order PDFs.

Each vendor is subject to its own privacy policy and contractual data-handling obligations. No information is shared with these vendors beyond what is necessary to perform the function requested.

4. Data retention

Operational records (work orders, sign-ins, payroll inputs, invoices) are retained indefinitely as part of Oneiro's business records and to meet legal recordkeeping obligations. Authentication tokens are stored only while the integration is active; revoking the integration purges them.

5. Security

Access to the Platform is restricted to authorized Oneiro personnel. Sensitive credentials are stored in encrypted environment variables or protected script properties. Transport between the Platform and third-party services is encrypted via HTTPS.

7. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the Platform's features or in the services it integrates with. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
